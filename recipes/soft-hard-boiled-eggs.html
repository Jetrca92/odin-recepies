Soft Hard-Boiled eggs

Ingredients:

  • 1 Â¼ cups water
  • 6 large cold eggs

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Place water into a 3-quart saucepan with a lid. Place over high heat and bring to a low boil. Carefully place eggs in the water. Cover pan immediately, reduce heat to medium-high, and cook for 9 1/2 minutes.
  2. Step 2> Remove pan from heat and cool eggs down with cold running water, tipping out the water, and continuing to run cold water over the eggs until they are cool.
